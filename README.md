The Automatic Chicken Door is a solo project utilizing an ESP32 microcontroller to automatically open and close the door for a chicken coop.

The ESP32 controls a 12V motor that is attached to a swing arm, allowing the door to swing open and close remotely at a scheduled time, such as 5 am to let chickens out, and 7 pm to secure them at night.

I have also built an iOS app you can access from the iPhone to manually open and close, change the time of operations, and control other settings.

Parts List: ESP32 microcontroller, DRV8871 DC motor driver, 12V worm gear motor, IAN219 current detector, BH1750FVI light detector, 12V to 5V Buck Converter, 2.4" OLED Display, Custom 3D Printed Utilities.
